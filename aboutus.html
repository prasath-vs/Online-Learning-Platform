Welcome to our online learning platform! We are dedicated to providing high-quality education through an accessible and interactive online environment. Here's an overview of our platform:

- User Registration and Authentication: Our platform allows users to securely register for a new account or log in using existing credentials. Upon successful authentication, users gain access to their personalized dashboard, providing a seamless and secure user experience.
- Course Management: Administrators can easily create, modify, and delete courses through an intuitive admin panel. Users can explore available courses, enroll in desired ones, and conveniently access enrolled courses directly from their dashboard.
- Payment Integration: Seamless integration with payment gateways enables users to purchase courses, granting access to premium content. A streamlined payment process unlocks exclusive learning materials, enhancing the educational journey.

- Notifications: Users stay informed about critical updates, course announcements, and upcoming deadlines through timely email or in-app notifications, enriching their learning experience.
- Rating: Users can provide star-based ratings for courses, fostering feedback and engagement. Community-driven insights help users make informed decisions when selecting courses.
- Quiz Game: Interactive quiz games enrich the learning experience, encouraging active engagement with course content. Users can earn award points or badges, gamifying the learning process and creating a dynamic environment.
- Personalized Learning Platform: Tailoring the learning experience to individual preferences, users can select their proficiency level (beginner, intermediate, expert). Courses are curated based on these preferences, ensuring relevant and personalized learning opportunities aligned with users' skill levels and goals.
